Who is this site for?
My site is for any person wanting information about me or my work.

Who is my target audience?
My target audience is future employers and future clients.

What is my site supposed to actually do?
My site is supposed to portray information about myself from a professional standpoint in order to give interested parties a place to go to get that information. My site is also a place where interested parties can get in touch with me, as well as see my work.

How will I achieve the target goal of my site?
I will achieve my goal with good, simple design and easy to read information.
